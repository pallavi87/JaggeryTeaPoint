Our Story

The Taste of Home – Naturally Sweet

Jaggery Tea Point is an emerging tea café concept that blends the comforting tradition of Indian tea with the wholesome goodness of jaggery. Unlike regular sugar-laden chai, we offer a healthier alternative by using natural jaggery as a sweetener, known for its detoxifying, immunity-boosting, and digestive benefits.

Our brand aims to not only provide flavorful refreshment but also promote wellness in every cup.
